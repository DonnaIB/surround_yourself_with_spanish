A little about us!

In order to take your Spanish language learning skills to the next level you have to surrounds yourself with the language.

We know it is not always possible to immerse yourself by living in a Spanish speaking country, but you can still can still immerse yourself by watching TV and movies in Spanish, reading in Spanish, essentially get as much Spanish input into your daily live as possible.

This is another fanastic idea that has come from our Dublin Intercambio community. We are always asked for recommendations of movies to watch or books to read to help improve your Spanish. The number of times we hear language partners discussing a new Netflix series or the latest movie they have watched. So we thought we would create a space where members of our community, and the wider Spanish learning community can share recommendations, rate them and provide an indication of the level of Spanish required to enjoy it!

What are you waiting for? Check out the recommendations, then register and create your own.
